Care Guide

Keep your jewellery beautiful forever

Your jewellery deserves love 🌸

Handcrafted pieces are delicate works of art. With the right care, your Bead n Beyond jewellery will stay beautiful for years to come!

💧

Water & Moisture

  • Remove jewellery before bathing, swimming or washing hands
  • Keep away from rain and humidity
  • Never soak beaded jewellery in water
  • If it gets wet, pat dry immediately with a soft cloth
🌸

Perfume & Chemicals

  • Always wear jewellery AFTER applying perfume
  • Keep away from hairspray and dry shampoo
  • Avoid contact with lotions and creams
  • Remove before using cleaning products
📦

Storage

  • Store in a soft pouch or jewellery box
  • Keep away from direct sunlight
  • Store each piece separately to avoid tangling
  • Keep in a cool, dry place
✨

Cleaning

  • Wipe gently with a soft, dry cloth after each use
  • Never use harsh chemicals or jewellery cleaners
  • For stubborn dirt, use a barely damp cloth and dry immediately
  • Handle with care — beads can loosen if pulled
📏
