FAQs

Everything you need to know

🛍️ Orders

How do I place an order?

+
You can order directly from our Shop page by tapping "Buy Now" on any product. For custom orders, visit our Contact page or WhatsApp us directly!

Can I customise a product?

+
Absolutely! We love custom orders. You can choose your own colors, stones, size and design. Just contact us via WhatsApp or the Contact page with your requirements!

How long does a custom order take?

+
Custom orders typically take 5-10 working days depending on complexity. We'll confirm the timeline when you place your order.
💳 Payments

What payment methods do you accept?

+
We accept all major payment methods including UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking and popular wallets through our secure Instamojo payment gateway.

Is my payment secure?

+
Yes! All payments are processed through Instamojo, a trusted and secure Indian payment gateway. Your payment details are fully encrypted and safe.
🚚 Shipping

Do you ship all over India?

+
Yes! We ship to all states across India. Shipping charges and delivery time will be communicated at the time of order confirmation.

How long does delivery take?

+
Ready products are delivered within 5-7 working days. Custom orders take an additional 5-10 days for creation before shipping.
💖 Products

How do I take care of my jewellery?

+
Keep your jewellery away from water, perfume and direct sunlight. Store in a soft pouch or box. Clean gently with a dry soft cloth. Visit our Care Guide for detailed tips!

Are the products as shown in photos?

+
Yes! All photos are of actual products. Since each piece is handmade, there may be very slight variations — which makes each piece uniquely yours! 🌸

Do you accept returns?

+
Since all our products are handmade and custom, we don't accept returns. However if your product arrives damaged, please contact us within 24 hours with photos and we'll make it right!
